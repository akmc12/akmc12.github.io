About Me

I am a researcher(PhD student) at the French Air Force Academy Research Center(CREA), working in collaboration with ANR FLEX HALE Project.

My research focuses on aeroelastic analysis of high aspect ratio wings, particularly flutter prediction and structural optimization of composite aircraft structures.

I develop computational tools for coupled fluid-structure simulations, combining finite element methods with aerodynamic solvers to predict critical flight conditions.

Technical Skills

Research Focus

Flutter Analysis

Time-domain and frequency-domain flutter prediction for flexible aircraft structures.

High Aspect Ratio Wings

Nonlinear structural analysis of very flexible wings using geometrically exact beam theory.

Fluid-Structure Interaction

Coupled aeroelastic simulations combining various Aerodynamics theory with nonlinear beam solvers.

Composite Optimization

Aeroelastic tailoring of composite wings for improved flutter boundaries.
